the heart behind the lens

hi, I'm Kristen

Not just your photographer

I'm a documentary family photographer based in Georgetown, Texas. I came to this work the way a lot of us find the things we love — slowly, and then all at once.

Becoming a parent changed the way I see a camera. Suddenly the photos I cared about most weren't the polished, posed ones. They were the in-between moments: the messy breakfast table, the bath-time giggles, the quiet of a toddler finally asleep on someone's chest. I realized those were the things I'd want to hold onto forever — and that most of us never get them photographed at all.

So that's what I do now. I document real family life, exactly as it is, so you can keep it.

what I believe

My approach

I believe the most beautiful photos aren't the ones where everyone says “cheese.” They're honest. A little imperfect. Full of the small details you'll forget if no one writes them down — the way your daughter's hair curls after a bath, the specific weight of a baby who won't be this small for long.

This is not a photoshoot. This is your life, beautifully documented.
