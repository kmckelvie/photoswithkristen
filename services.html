the mini session

$350 · up to 30 minutes

Short, sweet, and perfectly suited to busy seasons. Mini sessions are ideal for an updated family photo, holiday cards, or growing-up milestones. We'll meet at one outdoor location around Georgetown and make the most of beautiful light.

You'll receive 15 hand-edited images in an online gallery, with the option to add more if you fall in love with extras.

the family session

$550 · up to 1 hour

My most-booked session, and for good reason. A full hour gives us room to breathe — time for the posed-but-relaxed photos you want, and time for the in-between moments that end up being your favorites.

Includes a pre-session styling guide so you feel confident about what to wear, and 30 hand-edited images delivered in an online gallery.

day in the life

From $950 · documentary, in-home

My signature offering. A Day In The Life session is pure documentary: black-and-white, unposed, captured in your home as your real day unfolds. Pancakes and pillow forts, the chaos and the calm — all of it.

Choose a 1-hour ($950) or 3-hour ($1,650) session. The 3-hour is the truest record of this season of your family's life, with 100+ edited images.

This is not a photoshoot. This is your life, beautifully documented.
